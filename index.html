Welcome fellow traveler, we're delighted that you’re exploring Anytype, our local-first writing, organizing and collaboration tool.
It’s designed to give you more digital independence. Here’s what we mean:
Unrestricted Access: No one can prevent you from accessing your account, app or data.
Unparalleled Privacy: End-to-end encryption protected by your unique keys ensures that only you own your data.
Network Independence: Use Anytype in local mode and sync effortlessly within a peer-to-peer local network.
Beyond these core features, Anytype is a flexible, creative app where you can cultivate your own digital garden and share it with the people who matter to you.
